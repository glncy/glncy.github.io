I rebuilt this site from the ground up. The old one was a static page that listed a few things about me; this one is meant to actually _work_ for me — as a place to show what I build, share what I'm learning, and let people hire me.

## What changed

The biggest shift is intent. Instead of a résumé-in-a-page, the site now has:

- A **Projects** archive with the things I've designed and shipped
- **Posts** — long-form writing and quick TILs from day-to-day development
- A **Services** page and a real **contact form**, because I'm now taking on freelance work

## The stack

I went with **Astro** for the speed and the content tooling, **Tailwind** for styling, and a **git-based CMS** so I can edit everything without touching code. The lofi background and music player stayed — they're part of the vibe.
